About me

I am a researcher at UBICS, the Institute of Complex Systems, and the Department of Condensed Matter Physics at the University of Barcelona.

I study how data science can help understanding complex systems, and how our understanding of complex systems can influence data science.

Some aspects I am exploring in no particular order:

  • Deep learning and geometry for graph data.
  • Emergent synchronization in interconnected systems.
  • Diversity and diversification in interconnected systems.
  • Risk assesment with machine learning from financial data to climatic data.
  • Knowledge graphs and complex networks.
  • Science of science.
  • Machine learning explanations for complex systems.

I like to work with datasets of any size and complexity.
